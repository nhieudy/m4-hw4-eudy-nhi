Clairo: Singer and Songwriter

About Clairo

From Rolling Stone: Claire Cottrill was just 19 when she uploaded “Pretty Girl” under Clairo on YouTube, a song she wrote in a single day. With straightforward, relatable lyrics (“And I could be a pretty girl/I'll wear a skirt for you”) and delicate bedroom pop melodies, the track unexpectedly went viral. “It was just a little thing,” she told us, reflecting on the career-making moment in 2021. “It was not supposed to be anything.”

Clairo's Third Album: Charm

From Staged Haze:As she progresses as a woman and an artist, the 25-year-old is documenting her experiences in a sincere way, and delivering with the tone of someone that knows that this is just how it goes for everybody else, too. She has found her voice and her space in the music sphere and is just using both to share her story in the realest and prettiest way. Third time is a charm.

No Need for Labels

From Billboard: In a new cover story for Seventeen, the “Sexy to Someone” singer explained why she prefers not to put a label on her sexuality. “I do not care for labels at all. I believe people should like who they want and no one should have a problem with it,” she said. “I have had a better experience with my queerness once I just shut the ... up and let it happen to me. If I date a girl, I date a girl. If I date a guy, I date a guy.”

Why I Love Her Music

Clairo's music is emotional and raw. Her voice is soft and dreamy. It's the perfect music to put on to relax to, and I love working and reading to it as well. I hope to see her live someday!
